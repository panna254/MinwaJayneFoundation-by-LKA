    The Journey That Shaped Us

    Est May 2023

    Our Story

    Launched on Mother's Day 2023, the Minwa Jayne Foundation by LKA is inspired by a mother's care and compassion. "Minwa" means our mom in Luo, and "Jayne" is the founder's mother's name. We serve the world through the eyes of a mother — protective, kind, and ever-giving.

    Our mission is to spread love, uplift lives, and make a difference in the world. We believe in the power of kindness, compassion, and the importance of giving back to our community.

    Our Core Values

    What drives MinwaJayneFoundation by LKA

    Kindness

    Kindness is our currency. Whether it's a meal shared, a conversation offered, or a care package delivered, our acts are fueled by gentle compassion that sees people not as problems, but as precious human beings deserving dignity.

    Altruism

    We believe in giving with no strings attached. True change happens when support is offered from the heart — not for recognition, but because every life is worth investing in, especially those forgotten by society.

    Dignity

    We serve with respect and intention, restoring the dignity of young girls, elderly citizens, boys in need, and families on the streets. Every gift, every visit, every word of encouragement says: "You matter."

    Love

    The root of everything we do — from feeding the hungry to mentoring youth, our actions flow from a deep well of love that sees every person as inherently valuable and worthy of care.

    Program Highlights

    Our programs focus on empowering communities through practical support and heartfelt connection.

    Empowering Young Girls

    Supporting girls with sanitary towels, mentorship, and encouragement for confident growth.

    Supporting Boys' Development

    Empowering boys through mentorship and essential supplies like boxers and personal care items.

    Visiting Orphanages

    Bringing joy and comfort to children through quality time and donations.

    Caring for the Elderly

    Honoring elderly individuals through visits, stories, and shared meals.

    Feeding Street Families

    Providing warm meals and essentials to families in need.

    Lisha Rafiki (Online Unga Drive)

    Virtual outreach on TikTok Live for maize flour distribution and community upliftment.
