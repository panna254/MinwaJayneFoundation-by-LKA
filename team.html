Louisa Kimberly

Founder

Passionate about making a difference in the lives of those in need, Louisa founded MinwaJayne Foundation to honor her mother's legacy of love and compassion. With a heart for community and a vision for change, she leads the foundation in its mission to uplift lives through various programs and initiatives.

Her Journey

Driven by personal experiences and a deep sense of empathy, Louisa has dedicated her life to helping those in need. Her journey began with small acts of kindness that grew into a movement of compassion and support. Today, she continues to inspire and lead by example, showing that one person can make a significant difference in the world.

Founder's Message

"Every act of kindness, no matter how small, has the power to change someone's life. It's not about what we have, but what we give. Let us be the light in someone's darkness, for in giving, we receive the greatest gift of all - purpose."

- Louisa Kimberly
