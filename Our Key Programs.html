Our Key Programs

Empowering Young Girls

We support school-going girls by providing sanitary towels, hygiene kits, and mentorship sessions that encourage academic focus and self-worth. These efforts boost their confidence, reduce school absenteeism, and promote positive self-identity during critical formative years.

  • Monthly hygiene kits distribution
  • Monthly mentorship sessions
  • Academic support and guidance
  • Self-esteem workshops
Sponsor a Hygiene Kit

Supporting Boys' Development

In our commitment to holistic growth, we extend support to young boys through mentorship programs and the distribution of personal care items like boxers. These efforts help build confidence, reinforce values, and create a safe space for boys to express themselves as they navigate adolescence.

  • Personal care items distribution
  • Career guidance sessions
  • Life skills training
  • Sports and recreation activities
Support Boys' Program

Visiting Orphanages

We are dedicated to brightening the lives of children in orphanages by spending quality time, sharing resources, and creating joyful, healing moments. These visits remind them that they are loved, seen, and part of a greater community.

  • Regular visits and activities
  • Educational support
  • Creative arts programs
  • Holiday celebrations
Join a Visit

Caring for the Elderly

We honor elderly men and women in our communities through visits, conversations, and small acts of service that restore dignity and reduce isolation. Their stories matter—and we show up to listen.

  • Regular home visits
  • Companionship services
  • Basic needs assistance
  • Storytelling sessions
Volunteer with Us

Feeding Street Families

Many families face extreme hardship on the streets. We provide hot meals, basic supplies, and—most importantly—human connection. Every act of nourishment is a message: "You are not forgotten."

  • Daily meal distribution
  • Basic supplies provision
  • Health screenings
  • Family support services
Donate a Meal

Lisha Rafiki Online Initiative

This is a TikTok live outreach where we gift Unga (maize flour) to individuals online in need. We invite guests, musicians, and speakers to offer encouragement, prayers, and music. It's where compassion meets community—virtually.

  • Weekly live sessions
  • Unga distribution
  • Guest speakers
  • Virtual support groups
Catch Us Live or Give Unga Now
