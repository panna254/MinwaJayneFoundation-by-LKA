Our Story

Founded May 2023

MinwaJayneFoundation by LKA was born from a desire to make a tangible difference. Founded on Mother's Day 2023, it honors 'Minwa'—meaning our mom in Luo—and 'Jayne,' the name of the founder's mother. We serve through the lens of motherhood, offering compassion, dignity, and support. We believe that every person deserves access to love and basic needs.

Our Values

  • Love — The root of everything we do
  • Kindness — In our speech, our service, and our presence
  • Altruism — Giving without expecting anything in return

Our Mission

Motherhood-inspired service for Kenya's most vulnerable

"Driven by the heart of a mother, MinwaJayneFoundation by LKA serves Kenya's most vulnerable with compassion, love, and kindness. We believe every person deserves basic needs and dignity, and we strive to make a tangible difference through our programs and initiatives."

- Louisa Kimberly-Agunda, Founder

A compassionate Kenya where every person is valued and supported

"A Kenya where every person is seen, loved, and supported through compassion, kindness, and motherhood-inspired service. A community where basic needs are met, dignity is upheld, and no one is left behind. Through our programs and initiatives, we envision a society where love prevails and every life is touched by genuine care."

Meet the Founder

The driving force behind MinwaJayneFoundation by LKA

Louisa Kimberly

Founder

Passionate about making a difference in the lives of those in need, Louisa founded MinwaJayneFoundation by LKA to honor her mother's legacy of love and compassion. With a heart for community and a vision for change, she leads the foundation in its mission to uplift lives through various programs and initiatives.

Her Journey

Driven by personal experiences and a deep sense of empathy, Louisa has dedicated her life to helping those in need. Her journey began with small acts of kindness that grew into a movement of compassion and support. Today, she continues to inspire and lead by example, showing that one person can make a significant difference in the world.

Founder's Message

"Every act of kindness, no matter how small, has the power to change someone's life. It's not about what we have, but what we give. Let us be the light in someone's darkness, for in giving, we receive the greatest gift of all - purpose."

- Louisa Kimberly
