The Journey That Shaped Us

Est May 2023

Our Story

"Minwa Jayne Foundation was born from a desire to make a tangible difference. Founded on Mother's Day 2023, it honors 'Minwa,' meaning our mom in Luo, and 'Jayne,' the name of our founder's mother. We serve through the lens of motherhood—offering compassion, dignity, and hope. We believe that every person deserves access to love, basic needs, and opportunity."

— Louisa Kimberly-Agunda, Founder

Watch Video

Our Mission

Motherhood-inspired service for Kenya's most vulnerable

"To serve Kenya's most vulnerable communities through the lens of motherhood—offering compassion, dignity, and hope to every person, regardless of their circumstances. We believe that every individual deserves access to love, basic needs, and opportunity, and we strive to make a tangible difference through our programs and initiatives."

Motherhood

We serve through the eyes of a mother—protective, kind, and ever-giving, inspired by our founder's mother and the word 'Minwa' (Our Mom in Luo).

Empowerment

We empower youth through mentorship, education, and essential resources, helping them grow with confidence and dignity.

Community

We build strong, supportive communities where everyone has a role to play in creating positive change, from street families to elders.

Our Vision

A Kenya where every person is seen, loved, and not forgotten

"A Kenya where every individual—regardless of their background or circumstances—has access to the resources, support, and opportunities they need to thrive. A community where compassion drives progress, kindness prevails, and no one is left behind."

Empowering Youth

Supporting the growth and development of young girls and boys through mentorship, education, and essential resources.

Caring Communities

Building stronger communities by supporting orphanages, elderly care, and street families with love and resources.

Online Impact

Using digital platforms like TikTok to spread kindness, share resources, and create virtual communities of support.

Sustainable Change

Creating lasting impact through sustainable programs that address immediate needs while building long-term solutions.

Meet the Founder

The driving force behind MinwaJayne Foundation

Louisa Kimberly

Founder

Passionate about making a difference in the lives of those in need, Louisa founded MinwaJayne Foundation to honor her mother's legacy of love and compassion. With a heart for community and a vision for change, she leads the foundation in its mission to uplift lives through various programs and initiatives.

Her Journey

Driven by personal experiences and a deep sense of empathy, Louisa has dedicated her life to helping those in need. Her journey began with small acts of kindness that grew into a movement of compassion and support. Today, she continues to inspire and lead by example, showing that one person can make a significant difference in the world.

Founder's Message

"Every act of kindness, no matter how small, has the power to change someone's life. It's not about what we have, but what we give. Let us be the light in someone's darkness, for in giving, we receive the greatest gift of all - purpose."

- Louisa Kimberly
